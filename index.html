Hack Different

Wellesley Hacks is a twelve hour high school hackathon at Wellesley High School. Close to three hundred students from around the world will form teams of four to create awesome games, apps, or work on the latest hardware to make something awesome. However, Wellesley Hacks isn't about competing; it's about learning. That's why we've opened the event to anyone and everyone, even students who have never programmed before.

What is a hackathon?

Hackathons provide a venue for self-expression and creativity through technology. Students come together for a period of time, form teams around a problem or idea, and collaboratively code a unique solution from scratch — these generally take the form of websites, mobile apps, robots, or some new invention. Hackathons have a vibrant culture and promote an effort to create for good.

What if I don’t have any programming experience?

That’s fine! Wellesley Hacks is the perfect opportunity to learn to program. If you’ve never touched a programming language in your life, or want to pick up a new language, we’ll be having workshops that assume no prior programming experience by industry professionals. If you’re still not sure about attending reach out to wellesleyhacks@gmail.com, we’d really love to have you.

What should I bring?

A laptop (or desktop), toiletries, deodorant (please), and your Student ID (very important). Don’t forget chargers for all your devices and a powerstrip. If you plan on sleeping, bring a pillow and sleeping bag.

Can I work on my project prior to the event?

You are welcome to come with ideas beforehand, but all your work must be done on site (don’t worry, you’ll have 30 hours). All work eligible for prizes must be completed between Saturday after kickoff but before the expo on Sunday morning at the hacking location.

How much does Wellesley Hacks cost?

Wellesley Hacks is free, we’ll feed you, clothe you, and deck you out in swag.

Who can attend?

As long as you are a high school student you can attend Wellesley Hacks, even if you’ve never programmed before.

Will there be travel reimbursement?

We are hoping to have an answer to this soon. Subscribe to our email list (by clicking notify me when registration opens) for updates.

I want to sponsor Wellesley Hacks.

Awesome, shoot us an email at sponsor@wellesleyhacks.com.

I’m not in high school, can I participate at Wellesley Hacks?

This hackathon is only for high school students, however, you could definitely mentor! Click the mentor button at the top of this page.

But...hacking is bad right?

There are different kinds of hackers! The hacking at Wellesley Hacks won't be illegal, no illegal hacks are allowed. Our hackers code solutions to problems that they face or want to help the world overcome.

Have any other questions?

We’d be happy to answer them, email us at wellesleyhacks@gmail.com
